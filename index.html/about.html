About Joahness and his Photoblog

Joahness was the creator of this blog. He's a first year college student, studying in Consolatrix College of Toledo City, Cebu, Philippines taking the course of Information Technology or IT. This blog is all about photography. This includes the top 5 categories: Landscape, Silhouette, Portraiture, Archithecture, and Pets. The purpose of this blog is to convey the importance and how amazing a photography is in different perspectives. Photography is an important tool for preserving memories and telling stories. Images let individuals relate to one another and also shed light on the past. Taking up photography is great because it forces you to document your experiences. Joahness started interest in photography in the age of 17. He also tries to edit them as far as it look good. It adors him capturing different moments in life that were eternity.
